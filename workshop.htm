Modernizing the app: A tale from the trenches

 

David Paul Belanger was born and raised in the rural francophone town of Pain Court, Ontario, Canada and is fluent in English, French and Spanish. He currently resides in Argentina with his wife and 4 children.

A graduate of the University of Ottawa (B Com, MIS) and the IAE Business School (EMBA), he has been a ColdFusion web developer for over 20 years. His work with the Canadian Operational Research Society kicked off a lifetime of constantly analyzing all sorts of processes and looking for ways to make them more efficient.

Outside of development, he’s been fortunate to have worked as an analyst, project leader and product manager in several companies. These experiences and a whole lot of interaction with users led to a very thorough understanding of streamlined software design.

If you’ve got a warm cup of coffee in your hand and an hour to kill, go ahead and ask David about the history of trains in America – we dare you!

If you’re ever wondered what it takes to take over a legacy project and integrate ColdBox, have I got a story for you!
